RETRIBUTION

Laura dramatically escapes the clutches of detective Keith Sharp and changes identity. It is presumed that she died in the crash, along with him, but evidence is inconclusive. She enters the Kruger park with intentions of making her way through into Mozambique.
On her way she inadvertently runs into a gang o frhino poachers. To save her skin she joins them, but has she another agenda? Meanwhile the parents of James, the helicopter pilot she brought down to earth, are convinced that Laura is still alive and send a mercenary to track her down.
But is he tough enough to deal with her? Only when they finally meet will we found out...
