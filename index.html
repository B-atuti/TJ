North Rift safe for new school term, police says

Banditry crisis

The National Police Service has put n place adequate measures to ensure schools
in the banditry prone areas in the North Rift are safe when they reopen next week.
Every school in volatile areas has been assigned ten armed National Police Reservists[NPRs]
in addition to regular police patrols.
"The area is generally pacified and conductive for school opening and normal business activities,"
said Odero who addressed officers at;
  1. Cemorongion
  2. Kapindusum
  3. Mukutani
  4. Arabal areas
